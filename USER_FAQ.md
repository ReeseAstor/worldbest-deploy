## 1. Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple:

1. Visit the Ember homepage
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your information:
   - **Display Name** - Your public name on the platform (minimum 2 characters)
   - **Email Address** - Used for login and notifications
   - **Password** - At least 8 characters for security
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to your dashboard

### What information do I need to sign up?

You only need three things:
- A valid email address
- A display name (minimum 2 characters)
- A password (minimum 8 characters)

### Is my email address verified?

After signing up, you can verify your email address in **Settings > Account**. Verified accounts have access to all features and can recover their password if forgotten.

### Can I sign up with a social account (Google, Facebook, etc.)?

Currently, Ember supports email/password registration and magic link authentication. Social login options may be added in future updates.

### What are the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy?

By creating an account, you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Links to both are provided on the signup form. We recommend reading these documents to understand how your data is handled.

## 2. Login & Authentication

### How do I log in to my account?

There are two ways to log in:

**Option 1: Email & Password**
1. Click **"Sign In"** on the homepage
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

**Option 2: Magic Link (Passwordless)**
1. Click **"Sign In"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **"Sign in with magic link"**
4. Check your email inbox
5. Click the link in the email to sign in automatically

### What is a magic link?

A magic link is a secure, one-time login link sent to your email. It's a passwordless way to sign in—simply click the link in your email, and you're logged in automatically. Magic links:
- Expire after a short time for security
- Can only be used once
- Don't require you to remember a password

### I didn't receive the magic link email. What should I do?

If you don't see the magic link email:
1. Check your **spam/junk folder**
2. Wait a few minutes—sometimes email delivery is delayed
3. Verify you entered the correct email address
4. Click **"Try a different email"** to re-send
5. If issues persist, try the password login instead

### How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password:
1. Click **"Sign In"** on the homepage
2. Select **"Sign in with magic link"**
3. Enter your email and click send
4. Once logged in via magic link, go to **Settings > Security**
5. Click **"Change Password"** to set a new password

### Can I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Yes! Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. Enable it in **Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication**.

### How do I log out?

To log out:
1. Click your profile icon in the navigation
2. Select **"Sign Out"** from the dropdown menu

To sign out of all devices:
1. Go to **Settings > Security**
2. Click **"Sign Out All Other Sessions"**

## 3. Profile Setup

### How do I complete my profile?

After signing up, customize your profile:

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**
2. Fill in your information:
   - **Profile Picture** - Upload a photo by clicking the camera icon
   - **Display Name** - Your public name
   - **Username** - Your unique identifier on the platform
   - **Bio** - Tell others about yourself and your writing
   - **Website** - Link to your author website
   - **Twitter** - Your Twitter handle
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

### What's the difference between Display Name and Username?

- **Display Name**: Your public-facing name shown to others (e.g., "Jane Author")
- **Username**: A unique identifier, similar to a handle (e.g., "janeauthor2024")

### How do I change my profile picture?

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**
2. Click the camera icon on your profile picture
3. Select an image from your device
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF (recommended size: 200x200 pixels or larger)

### How do I update my email address?

1. Go to **Settings > Account**
2. Edit the **Email Address** field
3. Click **"Verify"** to confirm the new email
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

## 4. Getting Started with Your First Project

### How do I create my first writing project?

1. From your **Dashboard**, click **"New Project"** or the **"+"** button
2. Or find the **"Create New Project"** card and click **"Get Started"**
3. Enter your project details:
   - **Title** - Your book/story title
   - **Genre** - Select "Romantasy" or your preferred genre
   - **Description** - A brief summary of your project
4. Click **"Create Project"**

### What types of projects can I create?

Ember is optimized for **romantasy** (romantic fantasy) but supports various fiction genres. Each project can include:
- Multiple chapters
- Character profiles
- Story bible elements
- Beat sheet templates

### How do I organize my chapters?

Within each project:
1. Open your project from the Dashboard
2. Click **"Write"** to access the editor
3. Use the sidebar to create and organize chapters
4. Drag and drop to reorder chapters

### Can I import existing writing?

Yes! You can copy and paste existing content into the editor. Full import features for various file formats are in development.

### How do I set writing goals?

Each project can have word count targets:
1. Open your project settings
2. Set a **target word count**
3. Track your progress on the dashboard and project page

## 6. AI Writing Features

### What AI features does Ember offer?

Ember includes several AI-powered tools designed for romantasy authors:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Steam Level Calibration** | Control the heat level (1-5) of AI-generated romantic content |
| **Voice Fingerprinting** | AI learns and matches your unique writing style |
| **Genre-Tuned Drafting** | AI trained on romantasy tropes, tension arcs, and HEA pacing |
| **Beat Sheet Templates** | Romancing the Beat and dark romance templates |
| **Trope Recognition** | AI understands popular romantasy tropes |

### What are the Steam Levels?

Steam levels control the intensity of romantic content:

| Level | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | Closed Door | Fade to black, no explicit content |
| 2 | Warm | Light romantic tension, kisses |
| 3 | Steamy | Moderate heat, suggestive scenes |
| 4 | Spicy | Detailed romantic scenes |
| 5 | Scorching | Explicit romantic content |

Set your default steam level in **Settings > Writing & AI**.

### How does Voice Fingerprinting work?

Voice Fingerprinting analyzes your writing samples to learn your unique style, then matches AI-generated content to sound like you wrote it.

To configure:
1. Go to **Settings > Writing & AI > Voice Fingerprinting**
2. Enable **Voice Matching**
3. Adjust the **Deviation Threshold** (how closely AI matches your style)
4. Upload writing samples for AI to learn from

### What are the daily AI generation limits?

Limits depend on your subscription plan:

| Plan | AI Generations |
|------|----------------|
| Spark (Free) | 10 per day |
| Flame | Unlimited |
| Inferno | Unlimited |

### How do I use AI to help with my writing?

1. Open your project and navigate to the editor
2. Select text or place your cursor where you want AI assistance
3. Use the AI toolbar or menu to:
   - Generate new content
   - Rewrite/refine selected text
   - Get suggestions for scenes, dialogue, or descriptions
4. Review and edit the AI-generated content to fit your story

## 7. Subscription Plans & Billing

### What subscription plans are available?

Ember offers three plans:

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|--------------|
| **Spark** (Free) | $0/month | 1 project, 10 AI generations/day, Basic steam levels (1-2) |
| **Flame** | $39/month | 5 projects, Unlimited AI, All steam levels, Voice fingerprinting, KDP export |
| **Inferno** | $79/month | Unlimited projects, Team seats (up to 5), Custom voice profiles, Priority support |

### How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plan**
2. Review the available plans
3. Click **"Upgrade"** on your desired plan
4. Complete the payment process

### How do I manage my payment method?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plan**
2. Find **Payment Method** section
3. Click **"Update"** to change your card
4. Enter new payment details

### How do I view my billing history?

Go to **Settings > Billing & Plan** and click **"View Billing History"** to see all past invoices.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plan**
2. Click on your current plan
3. Select **"Cancel Subscription"**
4. Follow the prompts to confirm

Note: You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

### Is there a free trial?

The **Spark** plan is free forever with limited features. Paid plans may offer free trials—check the pricing page for current offers.

## 8. Settings & Preferences

### How do I change my theme (light/dark mode)?

1. Go to **Settings > Preferences**
2. Under **Theme**, choose:
   - **Light** - Bright interface
   - **Dark** - Dark interface (easier on eyes)
   - **System** - Matches your device settings
3. The theme changes immediately

### How do I customize the editor?

In **Settings > Preferences**, you can adjust:
- **Editor Font Size** - Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large
- **Editor Font Family** - Georgia, Arial, Courier, Times New Roman
- **Auto-save Interval** - Every 1, 5, or 10 minutes (or disabled)

### How do I manage notifications?

Go to **Settings > Notifications** to control:

**Email Notifications:**
- Email notifications (master toggle)
- Project updates and milestones
- Weekly writing reports
- AI writing suggestions
- Collaboration invitations
- Marketing and promotional emails

**Push Notifications:**
- Browser notifications
- Mobile app notifications

### How do I change my language or timezone?

1. Go to **Settings > Account**
2. Select your preferred **Language**
3. Select your **Timezone**
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### How do I set my default Steam Level?

1. Go to **Settings > Writing & AI**
2. Under **Steam Level**, use the slider to set your default (1-5)
3. This applies to new projects—you can override per-project

### How do I configure my preferred tropes?

1. Go to **Settings > Writing & AI > Genre Preferences**
2. Click on tropes to add them to your favorites:
   - Enemies to Lovers
   - Forced Proximity
   - Fated Mates
   - Slow Burn
   - Found Family
   - Grumpy/Sunshine
3. AI will prioritize these tropes in suggestions

## 9. Troubleshooting Common Issues

### I can't log in to my account

**Try these steps:**
1. Verify you're using the correct email address
2. Check that Caps Lock is off when entering your password
3. Try the **magic link** option instead of password
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser or incognito mode
6. Check if your email provider is blocking magic link emails

### I'm not receiving magic link emails

**Check the following:**
1. Look in your spam/junk folder
2. Add our email address to your safe senders list
3. Wait a few minutes for email delivery
4. Verify you entered the correct email address
5. Try using password login as an alternative

### The page won't load or shows an error

**Try these solutions:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl/Cmd + R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Disable browser extensions that might interfere
4. Check your internet connection
5. Try a different browser
6. Wait a few minutes and try again

### My changes aren't saving

**Check these items:**
1. Look for a "Save" button and click it
2. Check your internet connection
3. Look for error messages on the page
4. Try refreshing and making changes again
5. Enable auto-save in **Settings > Preferences**

### The AI features aren't working

**Troubleshooting steps:**
1. Check if you've reached your daily AI generation limit
2. Verify your subscription plan includes the feature
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact support if issues persist

### I accidentally deleted something

**Recovery options:**
- **Projects**: Deleted projects may be in a trash/archive section
- **Text**: Use Ctrl/Cmd + Z to undo recent changes
- **Account data**: Contact support for recovery options

### The website is slow

**Improve performance by:**
1. Closing other browser tabs
2. Clearing browser cache
3. Disabling unused browser extensions
4. Using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
5. Checking your internet connection speed

## 10. Getting Help

### How do I contact support?

For assistance:
1. Go to **Settings** and find the **"Need Help?"** section
2. Click **"View Documentation"** for self-help resources
3. Email: support@worldbest.app
4. Use the in-app help chat when available

### Where can I find tutorials?

- **Documentation**: Click "View Documentation" in Settings
- **Help Center**: Visit our online help center
- **Video Tutorials**: Check our YouTube channel for walkthroughs

### How do I report a bug?

To report a bug, please include:
1. What you were trying to do
2. What happened instead (the bug)
3. Steps to reproduce the issue
4. Your browser and operating system
5. Screenshots if applicable

Send bug reports to support@worldbest.app

### How do I request a new feature?

We love feedback! To suggest a feature:
1. Email your idea to support@worldbest.app
2. Include why the feature would help your writing workflow
3. Describe how you envision it working

### What browsers are supported?

Ember works best on modern browsers:
- **Google Chrome** (recommended)
- **Mozilla Firefox**
- **Apple Safari**
- **Microsoft Edge**

For the best experience, keep your browser updated to the latest version.
